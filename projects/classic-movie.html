Reflection and feedback
Reflection
In designing the Petition Analysis system, I focused on creating a user-centered platform that simplifies complex data while ensuring ease of use for government staff. By incorporating AI to automatically summarize citizen feedback, I aimed to streamline the decision-making process and improve efficiency. I prioritized clarity and usability, enabling staff to easily filter reports by date, category, and content, ensuring quick access to relevant information.

Balancing functionality with aesthetics was key; I integrated Taoyuan City’s visual identity while maintaining a clean, intuitive interface. This helped reinforce the platform’s alignment with the city’s brand without sacrificing user experience.

Throughout the project, I collaborated closely with stakeholders and developers to ensure that the design met both technical and user requirements. Continuous feedback and iterative improvements were crucial in refining the platform, reflecting my commitment to delivering a seamless, efficient tool for real-time data interpretation and effective policy-making.
