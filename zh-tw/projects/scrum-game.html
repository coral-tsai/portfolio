Scrum Game
In the Time Chamber, communicate with front-end developers online to complete the project together.
"太空豬！出任務！" is a interactive guide through gameplay. It is the third-week theme of the fourth season of The F2E Front-end & UI Challenge organized by HexSchool.

The event adopts a relay collaboration between UI designers and front-end developers, with different topics each week. Front-end developers can use the design drafts of UI designers to jointly produce a complete work.

User Story
  • I can learn about the roles involved in Scrum and their responsibilities from the website.
  • I can learn about the Sprint process from the website.
Design Concept
"太空豬！出任務！" Scrum team members are usually referred to as pigs, and the team roles are represented by a human in a pig suit + a space helmet. The welcome page incorporates elements of the company, serving as the mascot for TT Information and introducing everyone at the start.
Persona
User A
New employees who need to understand Scrum in order to join the team’s development.
  • Scenario:
    New employees must quickly gain a basic understanding of the Scrum process to establish a common language within the team.
  • Problem:
    The training materials are incomplete, and senior employees are busy with their own tasks, so they cannot immediately provide enough resources for the new employee to understand Scrum.
  • Solution:
    Provide them with the Scrum Beginner Village on THE F2E platform, allowing them to quickly experience and gain a preliminary understanding of the Scrum process.
User B
沒有聽過 Scrum，想快速上手知識點的任何人
  • 情境：偶然聽到或是看到 Scrum 這個詞，想了解流程與基本概念。
  • 遇到問題：資料太多不知道從何看起，文字說明比較生硬難讀。
  • 解決方案：提供給他 THE F2E 上的 Scrum 新手村，透過拖拉式互動，對每個階段的流程更有感受度。
